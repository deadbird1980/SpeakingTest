The News Service is the main communication link between the University and the news media. The office has a staff of writers, marketers, and photographers who generate and coordinate media coverage worldwide. The news service is one of three offices authorized to distribute news releases for the University. The others are the Agricultural Communication Service and Athletic Public Relations. In addition to distributing news releases via mail, fax, e-mail, and computer networks, the news service alerts media to events they might cover. It arranges news conferences, generates and coordinates news coverage for faculty who are making presentations at major conferences. It also offers private and group classes on working with the media, including radio and television.

The News Service monitors breaking news to spot interview opportunities for university experts. The other functions of this office also include providing e-mail summaries of media coverage to faculty and staff and coordinating media coverage with the Agricultural Communication Service and Athletic Public Relations. News service senior staff also serve as spokespersons for the University when the vice president for university relations is not available.
