Item 3 of 3: Pros and Cons

INTRODUCTION

As a teaching or research assistant, you will often find yourself in situations where you have to address problems and justify decisions.

TASK

Your task in the next item is to make a decision and discuss the pros and cons - both the good and the bad points -- associated with the decision.


Imagine you are a teaching assistant. Your student Mary came to talk to you in office hours, saying that she saw Jim, another student from your class, cheating on the last exam.


QUESTION

Talk about what you would do, and then discuss the pros and cons associated with your decision.
